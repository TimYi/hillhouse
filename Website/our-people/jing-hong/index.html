JING HONG

PARTNER


Jing Hong is a Partner at Hillhouse Capital Group. Prior to joining Hillhouse, Ms. Hong was a Managing Director at General Atlantic LLC, a leading global private equity firm where she was the head of Emerging Market Consumer sector and the General Manager of Beijing Office. Previously she worked as Senior Associate at Warburg Pincus LLC and Engagement Manager at McKinsey & Company.

At Hillhouse, she has led a number of deals in the e-commerce, consumer internet and retail sectors.

Ms. Hong received a B.S in Finance and a M.S. in Management Engineering from School of Economics & Management, Tsinghua University, as well as an MBA from Harvard Business School.
