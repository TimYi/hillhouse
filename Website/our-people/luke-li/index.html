LUKE LI

PARTNER


Luke Li is a Partner at Hillhouse Capital Group. He has worked with Hillhouse since the inception of the firm. Previously, he spent eight years at a technology company, where he held various positions including senior engineer, project manager, director, CTO and CEO of the company.

Mr. Li’s work at Hillhouse includes investments in prominent financial and industrial companies both in the public and private equity portfolio.

Mr. Li graduated from Tsinghua University with a B.S. in Engineering and an M.S. in Management.
