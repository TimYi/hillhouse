TRACY MA

COO | PARTNER

Tracy Ma is the COO of Hillhouse Capital Group. She has worked with Hillhouse since the inception of the firm. Previously, she was the manager of the internal auditing department at China Securities Co., ltd., a leading domestic securities firm.

Ms. Ma holds an MBA from the University Of Chicago Booth School Of Business, an M.S. in Management from the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and a B.S. from Beijing Normal University. Ms. Ma is a Chartered Public Accountant (CPA) holder.
