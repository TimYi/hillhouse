VINCENT GAO

PARTNER


Vincent Gao is a Partner at Hillhouse Capital Group. Prior to joining Hillhouse, he was a partner at ChinaRock Capital Management in Hong Kong, a division of Farallon Capital Management. Mr. Gao has been in the investment industry since 1996 and was also a portfolio manager and analyst at Citigroup Asset Management in New York, and an analyst at Bankers Trust Funds Management in Sydney, Australia.

Mr. Gao covers the internet and media space.

Mr. Gao is a CFA charterholder. He holds a Bachelor of Commerce/Economics Degree from the University of New South Wales, and has a Graduate Diploma in Finance and Applied Investment from the Securities Institute of Australia.
